Birth Doula

Our birth support package includes the following:

  • On-call support when your birthing time is near

  • Continuous support from one doula from when you need us in labour through the first hours postpartum

  • Emotional, physical, and informational support during labour

  • 2 prenatal visits to cultivate a trusting, comfortable relationship

  • Phone and email support during pregnancy and postpartum

  • Use of a TENS unit during labour

  • Referrals to practitioners in your community

  • Lending library for you to borrow from

  • 2 postpartum visits to review your birth, help you and baby establish feeding, and settle into your life together
