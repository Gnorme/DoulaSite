Services

Doula services provide continuous emotional, physical, and informational support throughout pregnancy, labour, and postpartum. Doulas advocate for the birthing person's preferences, offer comfort measures during labour, provide evidence-based information, and assist with postpartum care, which includes but is not limited to, sibling care, breastfeeding support, nutrition and anything the parents need to feel comfortable and confident. As your doula, I am here to be your personal resource throughout your pregnancy and the initial weeks postpartum.

    Once you have chosen to hire me as your doula, I find it important to meet with you in the comfort of your own home for two sessions, each lasting approximately two hours. During these visits, we will take the time to get to know each other better, discuss important information, and explore various options to help you prepare for labour and the arrival of your baby. We will also practice relaxation techniques, comfort measures, and different labour positions. I strongly encourage the involvement of your partner or anyone else who will be present during your birth.

    Starting from the 37th week of your pregnancy, I consider myself on call 24/7 to support you during your birth. My objective is to help you feel relaxed and confident throughout the process, so it is important for us to stay in touch regularly during this stage. Once you feel the need for additional labour support, I will join you and remain by your side until your baby is born and you are settled. Typically, I stay for 1-3 hours after the birth. During this time, I provide both physical and emotional care and support. I also ensure that your partner feels included by offering suggestions and encouraging their participation according to their comfort level. After the birth, my doula services include immediate breastfeeding support and assisting you and your family in settling in and preparing for rest.

Meet Barbara
